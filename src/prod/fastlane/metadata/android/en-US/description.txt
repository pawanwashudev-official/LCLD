🛡️ LCLD: Locate & Control Lost Device

Take absolute control of your device's security with LCLD. Whether your phone is lost or stolen, LCLD provides powerful, privacy-focused tools to help you find and manage it—even without an internet connection.

Why choose LCLD?

Most device tracking apps rely on proprietary services and constant data connections. LCLD is built for independence, privacy, and reliability. It works seamlessly via SMS commands, making it the perfect solution for offline situations or de-Googled devices.

Key Features:

📍 INSTANT LOCATION: Get real-time GPS coordinates via SMS or web.
🔊 FORCED RINGING: Find your phone even if it's on Silent or DND.
📸 REMOTE SNAPSHOTS: Use the front or back camera to see surroundings or identify who has your device.
🔒 REMOTE LOCKING: Secure your phone with a custom message and PIN.
🧹 SECURE WIPE: Protect your privacy with a full remote factory reset.
📡 OFFLINE CONTROL: No internet? No problem. Full control via SMS.
🔒 PRIVACY FIRST: Open-source and self-hostable. Your location is your business.

Usage:

Control LCLD easily via SMS or through a secure web interface. 

For SMS control:
- Add trusted contacts for automatic authorization.
- Use a secure PIN to allow commands from any phone.
- Text "fmd help" to your phone to see all available commands.

For Web control:
- Connect to a public or self-hosted FMD Server for map-based tracking and photo viewing.

LCLD is the ultimate companion for anyone who values security and privacy.
